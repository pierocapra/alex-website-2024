About me

I am a passionate and versatile musical theatre performer with years of experience across various facets of the industry, including theatre productions and cruise line entertainment. My journey in the performing arts began at Motherwell College, where I honed my craft and developed a deep love for the stage. Since then, I've had the privilege of bringing diverse roles to life, captivating audiences with my dedication and artistry. Whether on land or at sea, I strive to deliver memorable performances that resonate with audiences and showcase my commitment to the art of musical theatre.
